# ADXL335-accelerometer

The y and z axis are swapped, lines 17 and 18 swap the y and z axis. This can bee seen in the image below.

- Pins:
- Vin to 5V
- 3v3 to AREF
- Gnd to Gnd
- Xout to A0
- Yout to A1
- Zout to A2
